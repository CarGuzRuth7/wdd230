Purpose

Our mission in Bountiful Foods is to bring the most healthy and organic products coming from the local farmers and national and international providers to your hand, ensuring the environment preservation and quality of our organic products free of agrochemicals through our service focused on the customer and providing them wherever they visit us, our fresh products prepared made to order. We count on our central in Carlsbad, California, our coastal truck service from Los Angeles to San Diego, managed directly by the owner, and our phone number and website to bring the best experiences and attend to the concerns and needs of our customers.

Target Audience

Our business tries to reach those people, customers, and other services interested in promoting and consuming organic products to lead and create a culture of healthy eating and those interested in defending and preserving the agroecological environment.
