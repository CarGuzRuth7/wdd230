Purpose

Our purpose is to promote the development of the local community and small, medium, and large businesses by fostering culture, education, and formalization in favor of the different sectors and contributing information and updates to the community. Also, improving the relationship between companies and their competitiveness as well as being an intermediate between governmental affairs related to the area.
