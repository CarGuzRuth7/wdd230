Target Audience

This chamber seeks to reach, mainly, those people, groups, and companies in the local community that seek to promote and organize their business or find out about the competition and opportunities in the area. In addition to motivating and inspiring the different groups inside and outside this chamber. We hope to reach them through different devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops and printed banners and flyers.

Personas

Persona 1: Matias is 25 years old, he finished his first certificate in Administration, but he works as a technician. He plans to create a metallurgy company and wants to learn how to certify and promote his brand.

Persona 2: Roberto is 47 years old and has an employment agency. He wants to organize an event for young professionals to know the local companies they could apply for a job position.

Scenarios

1) A person who wants to join the Chamber of Commerce to promote their new company in the labor market.

2) A non-local company looking to learn about the competition in the area.

3) Someone who wants to receive the latest updates and regulations.

4) A local company that has been around for many years and is looking to expand to other regions

5) A boss who needs legal advice to regulate his small business
